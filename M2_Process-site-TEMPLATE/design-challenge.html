Design Challenge

Using a worksheet from the Human Centered Design Toolkit, we determined what our design challenge would be for the non-profit we chose.

What is the problem you're trying to solve?

The lack of affordable access to healthy foods in Spartanburg.

The Design Question:

How does Ruth’s Gleanings help provide inexpensive, healthy, and nutritional foods to people who need it in Spartanburg?

The Ultimate Impact

The aim is to increase donations and support towards the organization, as well as to attract volunteers to serve the cause

Possible Solutions

Decluttering the organization interface, application of consistent promotion and design, simplifying website text, remove low-resolution adverts and replace them with better quality ones, implement hierarchy

Context and Constraints

TThe organization is small and highly dependant on donations; lack of funding, recipients outnumber volunteers, information on organization may not reach those who need it most

The Design Question Restated

“How can I redesign this website to attract more volunteers and resources, as well as reach needful populations?”
